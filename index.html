Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a case?

Open the Log tab and either tap the microphone to speak or type your cases in the text field. Use natural language — for example, "3 lap choles, all LMA, ambulatory" or "CABG with swan and TEE." Anesto's AI parses your input into structured case records for you to review and save.

Can I edit a case after saving?

Yes. Tap any case in the History or Log tab to expand it, then tap "Edit Case." You can modify the date, procedure description, and any descriptor chips.

Do I need an account?

No. Anesto works entirely offline without an account. All cases are saved locally on your device. Creating a free account enables cloud backup so your cases sync across devices and are protected if you lose your phone.

How do I export my cases?

Go to the Account tab and tap "Export PDF" or "Export CSV." The PDF includes a professional summary with case counts and category breakdowns suitable for credentialing committees.

Is my data secure?

Cases are stored in an encrypted local database on your device. If you enable cloud backup, data is stored with row-level security — only your account can access your cases. We never collect patient names or identifiers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does voice input work offline?

Voice transcription requires an internet connection (the audio is processed by a cloud speech-to-text service). Text input and AI parsing also require a connection. However, all saved cases are stored locally and accessible offline.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your device's Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Anesto → Cancel Subscription. Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple — we cannot cancel on your behalf.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to the Account tab in the app. You can delete individual cases from the History tab, or delete your account entirely from Account settings. Account deletion removes all cloud-stored data within 30 days.
